ABOUT US

Since year 2004, Extreme Computer Technologies (Pvt) Ltd. has been serving customers in both the public, private and corporate sectors; we have the technical resources and the expertise in both, hardware equipment as well as customize solution, and are one of the leading computer suppliers in Sri Lanka.
Our continuous growth is due to our flexibility in adjusting to the market needs and trends, and we offer you a total solution at a cost effective price. In addition, customer satisfaction is guaranteed due to our excellent post and pre sales service.
Our hardware solution ranges from the simple desktop computers to high-end dual-processor multi user systems, portable computers, printers, scanners, network products and peripherals.
